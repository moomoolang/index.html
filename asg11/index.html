The Aces: a Dodgeball team consisted of the best players

The Aces are a dodgeball team consisted of the best players across the Haikyuu universe. Although they are not really on the same canoncially in the Haikyuu universe, realistically this would be the best dodgeball team if they were an actual dodgeball team. Some of the members in this team are known to be aces of their groups or a really good player.

Kei Tsukishima

Tsukishima already is known to be a skilled volleyball player as a position of a middle blocker, excellent in blocking other players on an opposite team from scoring a point. He is currently 6 foot 2 inches height wise and would be a perfect fit for blocking the competing team from hitting another player, he would be a good blocker and placed in the middle. He would be a great fit because he knows how to play mind games and get each member on the team to be a better player, because he is competitive

Kotaro Bokuto

Bokuto, also known to be the ace of his volleyball team, is great at scoring points against the opposite team and serving. He would be a good fit statistically speaking as he was acknowledged as one of the top five aces in the country, barely missing the top three. Bokuto is currently 6' 1" height wise and would be a great fit to be a thrower as he is also good at serving.

Toru Oikawa

Oikawa, also like Bokuto, is known to be the ace of his own volleyball team and the setter usually, and is really good at competing against other people as he is good at reading people’s weaknesses and skills. Oikawa is currently 6'0”height wise and would be a great fit to be a sniper as he is good at reading people’s weaknesses and skills.

Testuro Kuroo

Kuroo, known to be the captain and a middle blocker, is good at scheming and strategizing against other competing teams, also fitting that archetype of getting into people’s heads and mind games. Kuroo is currently 6’2”height wise and would be good as a catcher as he can scheme against competing teams and lead the group to win the game.

Wakatoshi Ushijima

Ushijima, also known to be a captain, is known as a wing spiker and his team’s ace. He was referred to as the number one ace in his region and ranked among the country’s top three aces. Ushijima is not necessarily a good leader, but he is able to communicate with his team members bluntly with the hard cold truth, but with his skills he would be a good wing player. He is currently 6’2” height wise.
