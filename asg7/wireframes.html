The Advantages of having wireframe sketches over wireframe softwares

Its quicker, more flexible and isn't very distracting in the early stages of web-development.

The Disadvantages of having wireframe sketches over wireframe softwares

It's lower quality which means it is low fidelity, often is duplicate work, there is a huge lack of interactivity and limited collaboration ( as it is non-digital), which me it is not able to be edited or shared as much as you can with digital softwares.
