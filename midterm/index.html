Welcome to Bali

Bali is known to be an Indonesian island that is located in the westernmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands known for its tourism since the 1980s and as well for its great culture for Hinduism as well as volcanic mountains, clean beaches, and beautiful coral reefs. Perfect for travelers and for those that love to learn about different cultures and history.

Highlights & Factoids

  • Location: Bali is located in the Lesser Sunda Islands, East of Java and west of Lombok.
  • Population: As of since 2024, There is approx. 4.5 million people who live in Bali
  • Language: The official language currently is Bahasa Indonesian, Balinese (for naties) and Balinese Malay.
  • Famous For: Bali is known for their arts like traditional and modern dance, architecture, painting, sculptures, beaches, volcanic mountains, and temples.
  • Climate: As it is located in the tropics, Bali has an even climate all year round with an average of d 30 °C (86 °F) and a humidity level of 85%.
  • Popular Activities: The most popular activities to do in Bali are during the festival seasons like Nyepi (Hindu New Year) ,going along the Tegalalang Rice Terrace, Visiting Kelingking Beach for nature and photography lovers, and going to Uluwatu Temple as it is believed to be one of the six key temples believed to be Bal's main spirtiual pilars.
  • Cuisine: Balinese cuisine is very diverse but Pork is usually one of the most common ingridents found in dishes suc as Babi Guling, also with classics such as Nasi Campur (Rice with various different dishes like vegetables or meat) , Satay (Skewers), Gado-Gado (Indonesian traditional salad with rice cakes, tofu, peanut sauce, spinach, and more).
  • Cultural Highlights: Some of the cultural highlights you will find while visiting here are woodcarving, handcrafts, traditional dance (pendet, legong, baris,etc), gamelan performances (percussion orchestra music), and distinct temple architectures.
