Places To Eat in Bali

SeaSalt Seminyak

Oceanfront dining where modern seafood meets local flavors, paired with golden sunset views.

Lotus Blossom Café

Overlooking a temple pond in Ubud, enjoy Balinese coffee, tropical fruits, and vegan-friendly plates.

Warung Bambu Indah

Authentic Balinese family cooking in a bamboo hut — try the nasi campur and fresh coconut juice.
