Design Statement

I chose Bali as my destination because it resonates with me personally as I am of Indonesian-Chinese background. I also have family living in Bali, so it seemed like a nice spot to choose, The visual culture has that earthy tones, ocean blues, and golden light, which infact made me inspired to create a natural and easy on the eyes interface. The site uses a muted tropical palette of teal, sand, and sunset coral to evoke warmth without overwhelming the user. Each section has generous white space to create a calm, vacation-like feel.

Typography which was used is a combination of: Playfair Display for headings and Poppins for body text, balancing elegance with readability. The navigation is consistent on every page for intuitive interaction, and call-to-action buttons (“Book Now,” “Contact Us,” etc.) use rounded edges and strong color contrast for accessibility. Images are large, full-bleed Unsplash photos chosen to communicate immersion and authenticity.

Layouts use a card-based structure with subtle shadows and rounded corners to separate information visually. This approach reflects modern travel websites while aligning with responsive design principles.
