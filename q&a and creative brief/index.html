2. Storytelling to enhance better User Experience-- Why framing your project as a story is the best way to refine ideas.

By framing your project as a story, you are forced to organize your work around a beginning, middle, and end, who is the user, what problem they have, what journey/steps will help them resolve that problem, and what result or resolution do you wish to achieve. This clarity assists in narrowing the scope (you can see what is essential and what is extraneous), prioritizing, staying focused on the needs of the user not the features, and making the design coherent (so that all elements of the design serve the story). It also enables you to test consistency: when some section of the work does not fit into your narrative, it may not belong and it may require reconsideration.

Productivity Website Project Creative Brief

Problem

Individuals find it difficult to remain productive and to be regular in achieving their objectives. The existing tools are either not enough simple (checklists) or too complicated (complex feature-filled platforms).

Audience

College students, freelancers, and novice professionals who desire a clean and inspiring approach to planning things and their progress.

Competition

Notion, Trello and Evernote are trendy yet overwhelming. Simple to-do programs do not have any long term goals.

Key Messages

  • "Stay focused and on track."
  • "One uncomplicated space of goals and tasks."
  • "Your progress made visible."

Call to Action

"Start now - make your first goal today" to encourage users to start using the tool now.

How to Measure Success

  • Registered users and active users.
  • Goals created and accomplished.
  • UFeedback on productivity improvement by the user.
